# Working Agreement

## General

1. CI must be green
2. TBA
3. How about Tests
4. How about PRs
5. WB: Feel-stuck > 1.5h, let's jump on a pairing session/call on Discord
6. WB: open a PR in draft as you start work
7. WB: deploy at least 1 time per day
